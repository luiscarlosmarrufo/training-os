# Training OS - Exercise Library

## Main Compound Movements

### Back Squat
- **Movement Pattern:** Squat
- **Primary Muscles:** Quads, Glutes
- **Secondary Muscles:** Hamstrings, Core
- **Fatigue:** High systemic, High local
- **Spinal Load:** High
- **Best For:** Strength, Hypertrophy
- **Notes:** Foundation of lower body strength. Listed as best exercise for both strength and muscle growth.

### Bench Press
- **Movement Pattern:** Push
- **Primary Muscles:** Chest, Triceps
- **Secondary Muscles:** Shoulders
- **Fatigue:** Moderate systemic, High local
- **Spinal Load:** Low
- **Best For:** Strength, Hypertrophy
- **Notes:** Primary horizontal pressing pattern. Core upper body strength developer.

### Weighted Chin-Up
- **Movement Pattern:** Pull
- **Primary Muscles:** Lats, Biceps
- **Secondary Muscles:** Back, Core
- **Fatigue:** Moderate systemic, High local
- **Spinal Load:** Low
- **Best For:** Strength, Hypertrophy
- **Notes:** Superior vertical pulling exercise. Listed as best for both strength and muscle growth.

## Unilateral/Accessory Movements

### Bulgarian Split Squat
- **Movement Pattern:** Squat
- **Primary Muscles:** Quads, Glutes
- **Secondary Muscles:** Hamstrings, Core
- **Fatigue:** Moderate systemic, High local
- **Spinal Load:** Low
- **Best For:** Strength, Hypertrophy
- **Notes:** Excellent for addressing imbalances. Lower spinal load than bilateral squats. Listed as top 5 strength exercise.

### Romanian Deadlift (RDL)
- **Movement Pattern:** Hinge
- **Primary Muscles:** Hamstrings, Glutes
- **Secondary Muscles:** Back, Core
- **Fatigue:** Moderate systemic, High local
- **Spinal Load:** Moderate
- **Best For:** Strength, Hypertrophy
- **Notes:** Essential posterior chain developer. Focus on eccentric hamstring loading.

## Power/Olympic Movements

### Hang Clean
- **Movement Pattern:** Pull
- **Primary Muscles:** Traps, Glutes
- **Secondary Muscles:** Hamstrings, Quads, Back
- **Fatigue:** High systemic, Moderate local
- **Spinal Load:** Moderate
- **Best For:** Power, Strength
- **Notes:** Develops explosive power and coordination. Often combined with Front Squat and Push Press in complexes.

## Rehabilitation/Prehab Exercises

### Tibialis Raise
- **Movement Pattern:** Mobility
- **Primary Muscles:** Tibialis Anterior
- **Fatigue:** Low systemic, Moderate local
- **Spinal Load:** Low
- **Best For:** Injury prevention, Rehabilitation
- **Variations:** Wall, slanted, seated banded, tib bar
- **Notes:** Critical for shin splint prevention. Can be trained daily if needed.

### Wall Angels
- **Movement Pattern:** Mobility
- **Primary Muscles:** Shoulders, Upper Back
- **Secondary Muscles:** Core
- **Fatigue:** Low systemic, Low local
- **Spinal Load:** Low
- **Best For:** Posture, Mobility, Recovery
- **Notes:** Diagnostic tool for shoulder mobility. Regular practice improves posture.

### Shoulder Taps
- **Movement Pattern:** Core
- **Primary Muscles:** Core, Shoulders
- **Secondary Muscles:** Chest, Triceps
- **Fatigue:** Moderate systemic, Moderate local
- **Spinal Load:** Low
- **Best For:** Core stability, Anti-rotation training
- **Notes:** Focus on zero torso movement. Quality over speed.
