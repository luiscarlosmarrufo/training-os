# Performance Enhancement Techniques

## Cool Hands Between Sets (Huberman Protocol)

### The Technique
Cool hands between sets to increase strength performance

### Implementation
- Use cold water or cooling device
- Cool palms between sets
- 30-60 seconds of cooling
- Don't cool to point of discomfort

### Mechanism
- Palms have special blood vessels (AVAs)
- Cooling reduces core temperature slightly
- Prevents overheating-related strength decline
- Can improve subsequent set performance

### Best Used For
- Heavy strength training
- High-intensity intervals
- Hot training environments
- Extended training sessions

## 50 Jumps Upon Waking

### The Protocol
Perform 50 jumps immediately after waking

### Benefits
- Activates nervous system
- Improves alertness for entire day
- Enhances morning training readiness
- Builds daily movement habit

### Implementation
- Any jump variation (pogos, jacks, etc.)
- Before breakfast/coffee
- Barefoot if possible
- Progress to weighted if adapted

## Deep Squat Achievement Protocol

### From Deep Squat Position
Perform all movements from deep squat:

1. **Knee Push-Aways** - ×20
2. **Overhead Reaches** - ×20
3. **Forward Folds** - ×10
4. **Internal Rotations** - ×10
5. **Squat to Forward Fold** - ×10

### Purpose
- Develops squat mobility
- Improves hip function
- Builds position-specific strength
- Enhances movement quality

## Spine Decompression

### Method
Use inversion boots for spinal decompression

### Protocol
- 2-5 minutes inverted
- 1-2× daily
- After heavy loading
- Before bed optimal

### Benefits
- Reduces compression
- Improves disc health
- May increase height temporarily
- Reduces back pain

---

## Landmine Press for Neck Pain

### Application
Landmine press as primary pressing when neck pain present

### Why It Works
- Angled bar path reduces neck strain
- Allows natural scapular movement
- Less shoulder impingement
- Can load heavily with less stress

## Bulletproof Back Goals

### Long-Term Standards
1. Back extension to 20% bodyweight
2. QL extension to 20% bodyweight
3. 30 knee raises without swinging
4. L-sit for 30 seconds effortlessly
5. Couch stretch with back to wall
6. Incline pigeon as rest position

### Achieving These Prevents
- Chronic back pain
- Movement limitations
- Training interruptions
- Age-related decline

## Three Points of Foot Contact

### During ALL Lower Body Training
Maintain contact with:
1. Heel
2. Base of big toe
3. Base of little toe

### Benefits
- Optimal force transfer
- Injury prevention
- Better balance
- Improved performance
