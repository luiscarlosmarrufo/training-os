# Back Squat

## Description
Fundamental lower body compound exercise performed with barbell on upper back. Primary strength builder for lower body.

## Key Cues
- Maintain tight upper back
- Drive knees out in line with toes
- Hip crease below knee at bottom
- Drive through whole foot
- Maintain neutral spine

## Common Progressions
- Main Lift 4-Week Wave
- Back Squat 8-Week Progression

## Notes
- Listed as one of best exercises for both strength and muscle growth
- High systemic and spinal demands require proper recovery
