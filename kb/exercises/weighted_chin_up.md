# Weighted Chin-Up

## Description
Vertical pull exercise with supinated grip, performed with additional weight. Superior for back and bicep development.

## Key Cues
- Full hang at bottom
- Pull until chin clears bar
- Control the descent
- Maintain hollow body position

## Common Progressions
- Main Lift 4-Week Wave

## Notes
- Listed as best exercise for both strength and muscle growth
- Supinated grip increases bicep involvement
