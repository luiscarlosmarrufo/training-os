# Bench Press

## Description
Primary horizontal push exercise performed lying on bench with barbell. Foundation of upper body pressing strength.

## Key Cues
- Maintain arch in upper back
- Shoulder blades pulled back and down
- Feet firmly planted
- Lower with control to chest
- Press in slight arc back toward face

## Common Progressions
- Main Lift 4-Week Wave

## Notes
- Core upper body strength exercise
