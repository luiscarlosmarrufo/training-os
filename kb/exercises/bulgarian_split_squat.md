# Bulgarian Split Squat

## Description
Unilateral squat variation with rear foot elevated on bench. Excellent for addressing imbalances and building single-leg strength.

## Key Cues
- Front foot far enough forward for vertical shin at bottom
- Keep torso upright
- Drive through front heel
- Control the descent

## Common Progressions
- Bulgarian Split Squat Weekly Progression
- 3×8 → 3×5 over 6 weeks

## Notes
- Listed as one of best exercises for both strength and muscle growth
- Lower spinal load than bilateral squats
- Can be loaded with dumbbells or barbell
