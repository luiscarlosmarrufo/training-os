# SW7 Academy Program

## Weekly Layout (7 days)

- **Day 1:** FBB Lower (Strength focus)
- **Day 2:** FBB Push (Strength + Capacity)
- **Day 3:** FBB Pull + Core
- **Day 4:** Rest
- **Day 5:** FBB Full Body (Strength + Engine)
- **Day 6:** VO₂ Max Intervals (Running)
- **Day 7:** Rest

## Day Structure

### Day 1 - FBB Lower (Strength)

#### Warmup/Prehab Circuit
1. Squat to Stand - 2×10
2. BW Single-Leg Glute Bridge - 2×8 each
3. Long Lever Lunge + Twisted Lizard - 2×6 each

#### Main Lift
- Back Squat - 4-week wave progression

#### Posterior Chain
- Barbell RDL - 3×8

#### Accessories (Superset)
- Nordics - 3×4
- Landmine Front Squat - 3×8

#### Finisher (3 rounds)
1. DB Step-Up - 20 reps total
2. BB Thruster - 15 reps
3. Watt Bike - 30 sec hard

### Day 2 - FBB Push (Strength + Capacity)

#### Prehab/Warmup
1. Prone Swimmers - 2×10
2. Shoulder Taps - 2×10
3. Half-Kneeling Banded Face Pulls - 2×10

#### Main Lift
- Bench Press - 4-week wave progression

#### Vertical Press
- Seated Overhead Barbell Press - weekly volume progression

#### Accessories (Superset)
- Cable Fly - 3×12-15
- DB Lateral Raise - 3×12-15

#### Triceps
- Rope Triceps Extension - 2×20

#### Finisher (5 rounds)
- SkiErg - 12 calories
- Press-Ups - max reps

### Day 3 - FBB Pull + Core

#### Prehab
1. Prone Swimmers - 2×10
2. Half-Kneeling Banded Face Pulls - 2×10
3. Chin Retraction - 2×8

#### Main Lift
- Weighted Chin-Up - 4-week wave progression

#### Horizontal Pull
- Barbell Row - 3×8

#### Accessories (Superset)
- Wide-Grip Lat Pulldown - 3×12-15
- DB One-Arm Row - 3×12-15 each

#### Gun Show
- Barbell Biceps Curls - 4×20

#### Core Circuit
- Plate Sit-Up - 10 reps
- Lateral Plank Dips - 10 each side
- Toe Taps - 10 reps

### Day 5 - FBB Full Body (Strength + Engine)

#### Prehab
1. Squat to Stand - 2×10
2. Prone Swimmers - 2×10
3. Quadruped T-Spine Rotation - 2×8 each
4. Hip Flexor T-Spine Rotation - 2×6 each

#### Strength Complex (5 rounds)
- Hang Clean - 2 reps
- Front Squat - 2 reps
- BB Push Press - 1 rep

#### Engine Circuit (3 rounds)
1. BB Thruster - 15 reps
2. DB Push Press - 12 reps
3. Pull-Up - 9 reps
4. Chest-to-Floor Burpee - 6 reps
5. Run - 400m

#### Midline Smash (3 rounds)
- Plate Russian Twists - 10 each side
- Hanging Knee Raises - 15 reps
- Sprint Plank - 30 sec

### Day 6 - VO₂ Max Intervals (Running)

#### Movement Prep
- Rocking Adductor - 2×10 each
- Single-Leg Glute Bridge off Bench - 2×10 each
- DB Forward Lunge - 2×8 each
- Hip Flexor Stretch w/ Rotation - 2×10 each

#### Warm-Up
- Run - 800m easy

#### Working Set
- 5 × 4 minutes at tempo/VO₂ effort
- Rest: 2:00 easy jog/walk between

## Notes
- Uses 4-week wave progression for main lifts
- Combines strength, capacity, and endurance work
- Structured recovery with 2 rest days per week
