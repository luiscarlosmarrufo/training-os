# Boxing Core Training

## Overview
Specialized core routine for boxers to develop the ability to take body shots and maintain core stability during fights.

## Body Shot Resistance Circuit

### The Gauntlet (No Rest Between Exercises)

1. **Half Crunches** - 50 reps
2. **Full Crunches** - 30 reps
3. **Heel Taps** - 25 each side
4. **V-Sits** - 20 reps
5. **Flutter Kicks** - 30 reps
6. **Scissor Kicks** - 30 reps
7. **Leg Raises** - 30 reps
8. **Russian Twists** - 25 each side (10kg plate)
9. **Sit-Ups with Plate Overhead** - 30 reps
10. **Plate Side Raises** - 25 reps
    - Arms extended throughout
    - Minimal torso twist
    - Hold plate, raise side to side

**Rest 2-3 minutes, repeat 2-3 rounds**

## Advanced Core Variations

### Bench Bridge Plate Transfer
- Head on bench, body in bridge position (legs 90°)
- Hold plate/DB with extended arms
- Touch floor side to side
- 3×15-20 each side
- Builds rotational strength and stability

### Weighted Core Work
- All exercises can progress with added weight
- Start bodyweight, add 5-10 lbs progressively
- Maintain form over load

## Striking-Specific Core

### Rotational Power
1. **Medicine Ball Side Slams**
   - Rotate up from side
   - Slam down in center
   - 3×10 each side

2. **Plate Russian Twists** (Heavy)
   - 10kg minimum
   - Full rotation
   - 3×25 each side

3. **Hugging Ball Rotations**
   - With band resistance
   - Explosive rotation
   - 3×15 each direction

### Anti-Rotation (Taking Shots)
1. **Plank Pull-Through**
   - KB or DB pulled across body
   - Resist rotation
   - 3×10 each side

2. **Bird Dog Pull-Through**
   - More challenging variation
   - Lift opposite leg for difficulty
   - 3×8 each side

## Programming Options

### Option 1: Daily Body Shot Circuit
- Complete full gauntlet once
- Every morning or evening
- Build tolerance gradually

### Option 2: Pre-Training Activation
- Pick 5 exercises from gauntlet
- 1 round, moderate intensity
- Before boxing/sparring

### Option 3: Dedicated Core Day
- Full gauntlet 2-3 rounds
- Add advanced variations
- 2× per week maximum

## Progression Standards
- Complete gauntlet without rest
- Add 5 lbs every 2 weeks
- 3 rounds with 10kg minimum weights
- Hold planks during rest periods

## Recovery Protocol
- Ice abdomen after intense sessions
- Massage gun work (toward heart)
- Stretch hip flexors and obliques
- Allow 48 hours between intense sessions

## Key Points
- Consistency builds armor
- Breathe during exercises (don't hold)
- Quality reps over speed
- Progressive overload essential
- Combine with proper defensive technique

## Notes
- Supplement with bag work (body shots)
- Practice breathing when taking shots
- Core endurance equally important as strength
- Mental toughness develops alongside physical
