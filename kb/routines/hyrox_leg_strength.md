# Hyrox Leg Strength Workout

## Overview
Specialized leg workout designed for Hyrox competition demands, combining strength with endurance components.

## Main Workout

### A. Sled Push
**Structure:** 10m × 5
**Rest:** 60 seconds between sets
**Intensity:** Heavy (competition weight or heavier)
**Focus:** Maintain low body position, drive through legs

### B. Box Squat × Box Jump Complex
**Structure:** 4×6 reps (each movement)
**Execution:**
- 6 box squats (controlled, competition height)
- Immediately into 6 box jumps (same box)
**Rest:** 90 seconds between sets
**Focus:** Power transfer from strength to explosive

### C. Single Leg Hamstring Curl
**Structure:** 4×6 reps per leg
**Tempo:** 3-1-3 (3 sec down, 1 pause, 3 up)
**Rest:** 60 seconds
**Focus:** Hamstring strength for wall balls and running

### D. Single Leg Extension Hold
**Structure:** 2×50 seconds per leg
**Position:** Full extension at top
**Rest:** 60 seconds between legs
**Focus:** Quad endurance for lunges

## Conditioning Finisher (20 minutes)

### EMOM Style Circuit
Repeat for 20 minutes (6-7 rounds):

1. **Minute 1:** Bike 1km (max effort)
2. **Minute 2:** Walking lunges 30m (bodyweight or light KB)
3. **Minute 3:** Burpee to plate ×5 (step on plate)

**Notes:**
- Each segment should take 40-50 seconds
- Use remaining time for transition/rest
- Scale distance/reps if needed to maintain quality

## Alternative Leg Builder Exercises

### 5 Key Single-Leg Exercises for Hyrox

1. **KB Front Foot Elevated Split Squats**
   - 3-4 sets × 8-10 per leg
   - Front foot on 2-4" platform
   - KB in goblet position

2. **Reverse Lunges with KBs**
   - 3-4 sets × 8-10 per leg
   - Focus on glutes, hamstrings, stability
   - KBs at sides or goblet

3. **Bulgarian Split Squats**
   - 3-4 sets × 6-8 per leg
   - KB or barbell loaded
   - King of single-leg hypertrophy

4. **Cossack Squats (Supported)**
   - 3-4 sets × 6-8 per leg
   - Use box/platform to limit depth initially
   - KB goblet or barbell back
   - Builds adductors and hip strength

5. **Kickstand Squats**
   - 4 sets × 10-12 per leg
   - DB goblet or barbell
   - Great glute and quad builder with stability component

## Programming Options

### Option 1: Strength Focus (6+ weeks out)
- Complete full workout as written
- 2× per week with 72 hours between
- Progressively overload weights

### Option 2: Competition Prep (2-4 weeks out)
- Reduce sets by 1 on strength work
- Increase conditioning to 30 minutes
- Add competition-specific movements

### Option 3: Maintenance (In-season)
- Pick 3 exercises
- 3 sets each
- Once per week
- Focus on movement quality

## Recovery Protocol
- Foam roll quads, hamstrings, calves
- Stretch hip flexors and adductors
- Ice bath or cold plunge if available
- Minimum 48 hours before next leg session

## Key Points
- Single-leg work crucial for Hyrox
- Balance strength with endurance
- Practice transitions under fatigue
- Mimic competition movements
- Progressive overload still applies

## Performance Standards
- Sled push: Body weight for 50m continuously
- Box squat: 1.5× bodyweight
- Walking lunges: 50m with 50% bodyweight
- Single leg strength: Within 10% of other leg
