# Bulgarian Split Squat Weekly Progression

## Pattern (3 sets each week)
- **Week 1:** 3×8
- **Week 2:** 3×8
- **Week 3:** 3×7
- **Week 4:** 3×6
- **Week 5:** 3×6
- **Week 6:** 3×5

## Implementation
- Performed as superset with glute-ham raises
- Use dumbbells for loading
- Rest 60-120 seconds between supersets
- Increase weight when reps decrease

## Notes
- Rep reduction allows for progressive loading
- Two weeks at 8 and 6 reps for adaptation
- Designed for unilateral strength development
