# Sprint 8-Week Progression

## Rest Formula
**yards ÷ 10 = minutes rest**
- 15 yards = 1.5 min rest
- 20 yards = 2 min rest
- 30 yards = 3 min rest
- 40 yards = 4 min rest

## Weekly Progression
- **Week 1:** 3×10, 3×15, 4×20
- **Week 2:** 5×15, 5×20
- **Week 3:** 3×15, 4×20, 2×30
- **Week 4:** 3×15, 3×20, 3×30
- **Week 5:** 4×20, 4×30
- **Week 6:** 4×20, 5×30
- **Week 7:** 8×30
- **Week 8:** 6×40

## Notes
- Progressive increase in both volume and distance
- Week 7 peaks volume at same distance
- Week 8 introduces longer sprints with reduced volume
- Designed for speed/power development
