# VO2 Max Intervals Protocol

## Steps
1. Perform thorough warm-up (800m easy jog recommended)
2. Run at tempo/VO2 effort for prescribed time
3. Active recovery between intervals
4. Repeat for prescribed number of intervals
5. Cool down with easy movement

## Standard Format
- **Work:** 4 minutes at tempo/VO2 effort
- **Rest:** 2 minutes easy jog/walk
- **Sets:** 5 intervals

## Cues
- Maintain consistent pace across all intervals
- Effort should feel hard but sustainable
- Focus on breathing rhythm
- Stay relaxed in upper body

## Common Applications
- Improving aerobic capacity
- Race preparation
- Metabolic conditioning
- Cardiovascular health

## Avoid
- Starting too fast
- Complete rest between intervals (keep moving)
- Skipping warm-up
- Training at this intensity more than 2x per week
